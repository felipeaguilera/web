Notebook

Recent writing.

I write about where culture, technology, and brand meet, usually starting from something I can't stop thinking about, a film, a launch, a small shift in how we live, and following it until it says something useful. I'm less interested in hot takes than in figuring things out in the open. It's how I think, and the part of the week I enjoy most. New pieces land most Fridays.

The longer story

Long enough at this to know what actually matters.

I led marketing and customer experience at Zmart for a decade, and built and edited its magazine, Inzider, along the way. Before that, digital projects for Metro de Santiago, Sodimac, Tetra Pak, and Codelco. Today I work independently through EVO Creative Lab, taking on brand strategy, content, and digital work for clients across sectors. I'm in Santiago, bilingual, and built for remote work, and I write because it's how I think.
